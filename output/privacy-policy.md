# Privacy Policy
**AI Finance Ops**
*Last updated: June 02, 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

AI Finance Ops ("we", "us", "our") operates **aifinanceops.app**, a SaaS platform for automating financial reporting for online businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and financial data.

By using our service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

**Contact:** privacy@aifinanceops.app
**Security:** security@aifinanceops.app

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, password (hashed)
- **Workspace details:** Company name, billing email
- **Payment information:** Processed by LemonSqueezy — we never store raw card data

### 2.2 Financial Data (via Integrations)
- **Bank data via Plaid:** Account balances, transaction history, institution name — used solely to generate your financial reports
- **PayPal data:** Revenue, payouts, transaction summaries — accessed via PayPal API with your authorization

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Data
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, features used, session duration
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, device type
- **Error logs:** Anonymized crash reports via Sentry

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---|---|
| Provide and operate the service | Contractual necessity |
| Generate financial reports and analytics | Contractual necessity |
| Send transactional emails (receipts, alerts) | Contractual necessity |
| Improve product features | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with legal obligations | Legal requirement |
| Send optional product updates | Consent (opt-in) |

We **never** sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

- **Plaid** — to connect and retrieve your bank account data. Plaid's Privacy Policy: plaid.com/legal
- **PayPal** — to retrieve your PayPal transaction data
- **LemonSqueezy** — to process subscription payments
- **Supabase** — our database provider (data stored in EU/US with encryption)
- **Vercel** — our hosting provider
- **Sentry** — anonymized error monitoring

All third parties are contractually bound to process data only for the stated purpose.

---

## 5. Data Security

- All data is transmitted over **TLS 1.3**
- Data at rest is encrypted with **AES-256**
- Plaid access tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- We enforce MFA for all internal system access
- Vulnerability scans run automatically on every code update

---

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Financial reports | 12 months (or until deleted by user) |
| Transaction data from Plaid | 90 days rolling window |
| Logs & error reports | 30 days |
| Billing records | 7 years (legal requirement) |

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

- **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction** — Request corrections to inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Portability** — Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — Object to certain types of data processing
- **Withdraw consent** — Revoke any previously granted permissions

To exercise any right, email: **privacy@aifinanceops.app**
We respond within **30 days**.

## 8. Cookies

- **Analytics cookies:** Used to understand usage patterns (opt-out available)

## 9. Third-Party Financial Data (Plaid)

When you connect your bank account via Plaid:
- We access only the data necessary to generate your reports
- We do not sell or share your financial data with any third parties
- You can disconnect your bank account at any time from Settings
- Disconnecting removes our access token — Plaid retains data per their own policy
