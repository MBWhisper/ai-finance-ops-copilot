Baremetrics is one of the most well-known SaaS analytics tools, but at $308/month for the basic plan, it's expensive for early-stage founders. If you're pre-seed to Series A and watching every dollar, there are excellent Baremetrics alternatives that give you MRR tracking, churn analysis, and forecasting without the enterprise price tag.

This guide compares the **five best Baremetrics alternatives in 2026** — with honest pros, cons, and pricing for each.

## Why Founders Look for a Baremetrics Alternative

The most common reasons founders look for a Baremetrics alternative:

- **Price** — $308/month is steep when you're at $2K–$10K MRR
- **No AI insights** — Baremetrics shows dashboards but doesn't explain *why* metrics change
- **No free plan** — only a 14-day trial before you're paying full price
- **Cash flow forecasting is limited** — not built for early-stage runway planning

## Baremetrics Alternatives: The Landscape in 2026

The subscription analytics space has matured significantly. The main Baremetrics alternatives fall into three categories:

1. **Dedicated SaaS analytics tools** — AI Finance Ops, ChartMogul, ProfitWell
2. **All-in-one payment + analytics platforms** — Paddle Billing, Stripe Atlas
3. **Lightweight dashboard tools** — Firstbase, Crystallize

One important trend in 2026 is the rise of AI-powered analytics. While Baremetrics and ChartMogul give you dashboards, newer Baremetrics competitors like AI Finance Ops use AI to proactively explain what's changing in your business — turning data into decisions without manual analysis.

## #2 ChartMogul

ChartMogul is the closest direct competitor to Baremetrics. It offers subscription analytics, cohort analysis, and revenue recognition features.

**Pros:**
- Excellent cohort and segmentation analysis
- Strong API and integrations
- Revenue recognition features for accounting

**Cons:**
- Starts at $119/month (expensive for early stage)
- No built-in cash flow forecasting
- Steeper learning curve

**Best for:** Growth-stage SaaS ($50K+ MRR) that needs advanced segmentation.

When comparing ChartMogul vs Baremetrics, both tools target similar audiences but take different approaches. Baremetrics focuses on simplicity, while ChartMogul emphasizes data depth and API extensibility.

## #3 ProfitWell (by Paddle)

ProfitWell offers a generous free tier (forever free for core metrics) making it a popular choice for bootstrapped founders.

**Pros:**
- Free MRR, churn, and retention tracking
- Good benchmark data
- Strong fundraising reports

**Cons:**
- Limited forecasting capabilities
- Paddle acquisition means less independent development
- No AI-powered insights

### ProfitWell vs Baremetrics

ProfitWell's free tier is extremely compelling — you get MRR tracking, churn analysis, and retention metrics at no cost. But Baremetrics (and AI Finance Ops) win on forecasting and dunning.

**ProfitWell vs Baremetrics verdict:** If you need free basic metrics and don't mind limited forecasting, ProfitWell is a solid choice. If you want forecasting, dunning, and AI insights, use AI Finance Ops instead.

## #4 Paddle Billing

Paddle is primarily a payment processor with built-in analytics. It's a good option if you want to consolidate payments and analytics in one platform.

**Pros:** All-in-one payments + analytics, global tax compliance, good dunning.

**Cons:** Higher transaction fees than Stripe, analytics are less detailed, you have to use Paddle as your processor.

**Best for:** Founders who want to outsource global tax compliance and payments.

## #5 Firstbase

Firstbase offers a lightweight subscription analytics dashboard focused on simplicity.

**Pros:** Simple interface, affordable ($19/month), quick setup.

**Cons:** Limited features, no AI or forecasting, smaller integrations ecosystem.

**Best for:** Solo founders who need basic MRR tracking without complexity.
