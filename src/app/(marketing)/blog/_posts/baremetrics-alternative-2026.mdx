Baremetrics is one of the most well-known SaaS analytics tools, but at $308/month for the basic plan and up to $1,000+/month for advanced features, it's expensive for early-stage founders.

If you're pre-seed to Series A and watching every dollar, there are excellent alternatives that give you MRR tracking, churn analysis, dunning, and forecasting without the enterprise price tag.

## Baremetrics Competitors: The Landscape in 2026

The subscription analytics space has matured significantly. While Baremetrics was one of the first dedicated SaaS analytics tools, a number of strong baremetrics competitors have emerged that offer similar — and in some cases better — functionality.

The main baremetrics competitors fall into three categories:

1. **Dedicated SaaS analytics tools** — AI Finance Ops, ChartMogul, ProfitWell
2. **All-in-one payment + analytics platforms** — Paddle Billing, Stripe Atlas
3. **Lightweight dashboard tools** — Firstbase, Crystallize

Each category serves a different need. Dedicated analytics tools give you the deepest metrics. Payment platforms are convenient if you're willing to switch processors. Lightweight tools work for solo founders but lack depth.

What most founders actually need is a tool that tracks MRR, ARR, churn, and cash flow — without the enterprise pricing. Let's compare the top options in detail.

One important trend in 2026 is the rise of AI-powered analytics. While Baremetrics and ChartMogul give you dashboards, newer baremetrics competitors like AI Finance Ops use AI to proactively explain what's changing in your business — turning data into decisions without manual analysis.

## #2 ChartMogul

ChartMogul is the closest direct competitor to Baremetrics. It offers subscription analytics, cohort analysis, and revenue recognition features.

**Pros:**
- Excellent cohort and segmentation analysis
- Strong API and integrations
- Revenue recognition features for accounting

**Cons:**
- Starts at $119/month for 10 customers (expensive for early stage)
- No built-in cash flow forecasting
- Steeper learning curve than alternatives

**Best for:** Growth-stage SaaS ($50K+ MRR) that needs advanced segmentation.

### ChartMogul vs Baremetrics

When comparing ChartMogul vs Baremetrics, both tools target similar audiences but take different approaches. Baremetrics focuses on simplicity and a clean UX, while ChartMogul emphasizes data depth and API extensibility.

**Pricing:** ChartMogul starts at $119/month (significantly cheaper than Baremetrics' $308/month), but its pricing scales with customer count, so it can quickly become expensive as you grow.

**Feature comparison:**

| Aspect | Baremetrics | ChartMogul |
|--------|------------|------------|
| Starting price | $308/month | $119/month |
| MRR/ARR tracking | ✅ | ✅ |
| Cohort analysis | ✅ Basic | ✅ Advanced |
| Cash flow forecasting | ✅ | ❌ |
| Revenue recognition | ❌ | ✅ |
| API access | Limited | Extensive |
| Learning curve | Low | Medium |

The ChartMogul vs Baremetrics decision often comes down to whether you need revenue recognition features (ChartMogul wins) or cash flow forecasting and simplicity (Baremetrics wins). For early-stage founders, both are expensive options.

## #3 ProfitWell (by Paddle)

ProfitWell offers a generous free tier (forever free for core metrics) making it a popular choice for bootstrapped founders.

**Pros:**
- Free MRR, churn, and retention tracking
- Good benchmark data
- Strong fundraising reports

**Cons:**
- Limited forecasting capabilities
- Paddle acquisition means less independent development
- No AI-powered insights

**Best for:** Founders who want free basic analytics and don't need advanced forecasting.

### ProfitWell vs Baremetrics

The ProfitWell vs Baremetrics comparison is one of the most common questions in SaaS analytics. ProfitWell's free tier is extremely compelling — you get MRR tracking, churn analysis, and retention metrics at no cost.

**Where ProfitWell wins:**
- Price (free vs $308/month)
- Benchmark data against similar companies
- Fundraising-focused reporting

**Where Baremetrics wins:**
- Cash flow forecasting
- Simpler, more intuitive dashboard
- Dunning email automation
- Customer segmentation and insights

**ProfitWell vs Baremetrics verdict:** If you need free basic metrics and don't mind limited forecasting, ProfitWell is a solid choice. But if you want forecasting, dunning, and deeper insights, Baremetrics (or a modern alternative like AI Finance Ops) is worth the investment.

The ProfitWell vs Baremetrics dynamic shifted significantly after Paddle acquired ProfitWell — many founders worry about long-term commitment to a tool owned by a payment processor competitor.

## #4 Paddle Billing

Paddle is primarily a payment processor with built-in analytics. It's a good option if you want to consolidate payments and analytics in one platform.

**Pros:**
- All-in-one payments + analytics
- Global tax compliance handled automatically
- Good dunning features

**Cons:**
- Higher transaction fees than Stripe alone
- Analytics are less detailed than Baremetrics
- You have to use Paddle as your processor

**Best for:** Founders who want to outsource global tax compliance and payments, and don't mind being locked into Paddle's ecosystem for both payments and analytics.

## #5 Firstbase

Firstbase offers a lightweight subscription analytics dashboard focused on simplicity.

**Pros:**
- Simple, clean interface
- Affordable pricing
- Quick setup

**Cons:**
- Limited feature set
- No AI or forecasting
- Smaller community and fewer integrations

**Best for:** Solo founders who need basic MRR tracking without complexity.

AI Finance Ops is significantly cheaper (free tier vs $119/month), includes cash flow forecasting (ChartMogul doesn't have it), and offers AI-powered insights. Dunning (automatic retry of failed payments) is on the roadmap. Currently, AI Finance Ops focuses on analytics, forecasting, and AI insights. For dunning, you can integrate with Stripe's built-in smart retries or a dedicated dunning tool.

### Which baremetrics competitor is best for forecasting?

AI Finance Ops is the only baremetrics competitor that includes 90-day cash flow forecasting with confidence bands in its free tier. Baremetrics also offers forecasting but at $308/month. Neither ChartMogul, ProfitWell, Paddle, nor Firstbase include meaningful forecasting features.

Yes, ProfitWell's core metrics (MRR, churn, retention) are free forever. However, after the Paddle acquisition, some features have seen slower development. ProfitWell also does not offer cash flow forecasting or AI-powered insights, which are available in AI Finance Ops.
