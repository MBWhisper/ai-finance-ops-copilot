ChartMogul is one of the most popular subscription analytics platforms, but at $119/month for the basic plan and $179/month+ for advanced features, it's a significant cost for early-stage founders who are still pre-revenue or at low MRR.

If you're looking for a ChartMogul alternative that delivers MRR tracking, churn analysis, and cash flow forecasting without the price tag, there are strong options available in 2026.

## Why Founders Look for ChartMogul Alternatives

ChartMogul is an excellent product, but founders commonly look for alternatives because:

- **Price** — $119-$179+/month is significant before you hit $10K MRR
- **Stripe-only focus** — ChartMogul's PayPal integration is limited compared to its Stripe support
- **No AI insights** — ChartMogul shows you data but doesn't explain what changed or why
- **No cash flow forecasting** — ChartMogul is analytics-focused, not forecasting-focused
- **Complexity** — the interface has a learning curve for founders who just want simple answers

### 2. Baremetrics — Best for High-MRR SaaS

Baremetrics is the original SaaS analytics tool. It has the deepest feature set of any ChartMogul competitor — dunning, trial insights, segmentation, and benchmarking.

**What it does:** Full MRR tracking, churn analysis, forecasting, dunning, customer segmentation, benchmarking against similar SaaS companies.

**Pricing:** $308/month starting price.

**Best for:** SaaS companies at $50K+ MRR who can justify the premium price.

**vs ChartMogul:** More features overall, but more expensive. Better dunning and customer data.

### 3. ProfitWell — Best Free Option

ProfitWell (now part of Paddle) offers free MRR and churn tracking. The core analytics are genuinely free — they monetize through their Retain (dunning) and Recognized (revenue recognition) add-ons.

**What it does:** MRR tracking, churn analysis, customer segmentation (free). Dunning and revenue recognition (paid add-ons).

**Pricing:** Free for core analytics.

**Best for:** Founders who just need basic metrics and aren't ready for a paid tool.

**vs ChartMogul:** Free, but lacks cash flow forecasting, AI insights, and PayPal support.

### 4. Stripe Sigma — Best for Stripe-Only Users

If you process 100% of your revenue through Stripe, Stripe Sigma gives you SQL-based analytics directly on your Stripe data.

**What it does:** Custom SQL queries on your Stripe data, pre-built MRR and churn reports, scheduled reports.

**Pricing:** $0.02 per query (effectively very cheap for most use cases).

**Best for:** Technical founders who process all revenue through Stripe and want full data control.

**vs ChartMogul:** Much cheaper, but requires SQL knowledge and doesn't support non-Stripe revenue.

### 5. Mosaic — Best for Finance Teams

Mosaic is a strategic finance platform designed for CFOs and finance teams at growth-stage companies.

**What it does:** Financial planning, MRR analytics, headcount planning, scenario modeling, investor reporting.

**Pricing:** Starting at $2,000+/month.

**Best for:** Series B+ companies with a dedicated finance function.

**vs ChartMogul:** Much more comprehensive (planning + analytics), but requires a finance team to use effectively.
