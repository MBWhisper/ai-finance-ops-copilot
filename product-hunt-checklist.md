# Product Hunt Launch Checklist — AI Finance Ops

**Target date:** Tuesday (12:01 AM PT / 7:01 AM UTC / 8:01 AM GMT+1)

---

## Pre-launch (T-7 days)

- [ ] Create Product Hunt account and complete maker profile (photo, bio, links)
- [ ] Follow 10+ active PH hunters in SaaS/finance space (e.g. @awilkinson, @_abhi, @chrishlad)
- [ ] Verify your product URL (`aifinanceops.app`) works end-to-end
- [ ] Prepare Maker Comment draft (see Task 3 output)
- [ ] Prepare all 5 gallery screenshots (see Task 4 specs)
- [ ] Upload thumbnail (240×240) — screenshot from `thumbnail-preview.html`
- [ ] Post teaser on Twitter/X: *"I'm building in public and launching my SaaS finance tool on Product Hunt next Tuesday. Follow along →"*
- [ ] Grow at least 50–100 followers on your PH profile (interact with other launches)
- [ ] Email existing users / waitlist: *"We're launching on Product Hunt — your upvote would mean the world"*
- [ ] Set up a PH launch page preview (Product Hunt allows you to save a draft)
- [ ] Confirm tagline, description, and first comment are final
- [ ] Schedule Twitter posts (1 announcement, 1 thread, 1 thank-you)
- [ ] Join the [Product Hunt Makers Discord](https://www.producthunt.com/makers-dashboard)

## Launch day — T-2 hours (10:00 PM PT night before)

- [ ] Prepare browser tabs: PH dashboard, Twitter, email, Slack communities
- [ ] Notify your existing users / waitlist with the launch link (do this a few hours before to build early momentum)
- [ ] Post on relevant Slack / Discord communities (Indie Hackers, SaaS groups, etc.)
- [ ] Set an alarm for 11:55 PM PT (submission opens at midnight PT)

## Launch day — Submission (12:01 AM PT)

- [ ] Submit the product to Product Hunt
- [ ] Immediately post the **Maker Comment** (Task 3) — this is the most important text on the page
- [ ] Share the PH link on Twitter/X with a simple ask: *"We just launched on Product Hunt! An upvote would help us reach more founders 🙏"*
- [ ] DM 5–10 people personally (not mass spam — real, individual messages)
- [ ] Post in relevant communities: Indie Hackers, Hacker News (Show HN), relevant subreddits
- [ ] Answer every single comment within 30 minutes — prioritize engagement

## Launch day — During the day

- [ ] Check the page every 2 hours
- [ ] Reply to every comment thread with genuine answers (no copy-paste)
- [ ] Thank every upvoter who also comments
- [ ] Share progress updates on Twitter: *"We're #6 in [category] today! 🎉"*
- [ ] Engage with other products launching the same day (sincere, not spammy)
- [ ] Update your profile / website to mention the PH launch

## Post-launch (T+1 day)

- [ ] Write a launch retrospective — what worked, what didn't, numbers
- [ ] Email everyone who signed up through PH: *"Thanks for your support — here's what's next"*
- [ ] Update the PH badge with the real post_id
- [ ] Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to the website footer
- [ ] Follow up with any investors / partners who saw the launch
- [ ] Analyze traffic sources and signup conversion from PH
